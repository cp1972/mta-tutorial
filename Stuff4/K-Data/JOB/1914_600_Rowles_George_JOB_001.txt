First job was as a telegraph messenger (aged 13 - 14 1/2), then ‘fiddled about at various jobs’ until apprenticed to local printer. Released from apprenticeship at age 19 but remained in the printing industry, except for period of military service (1914-19), in various capacities including compositor
 editorial proof-reader
 freelance journalist and writer
 London correspondent for Newspaper News (for 14 years) and editorial work.
