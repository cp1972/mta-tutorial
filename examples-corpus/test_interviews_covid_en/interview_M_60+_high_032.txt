Q: What lessons do you take from this period?
R: The vaccination process was well organized in my area, very efficient. Coordinating projects across distributed teams was a real shift. I went weeks without seeing anyone in person, only voices on the phone.

R: We invested in better technology to make remote work more comfortable. The emotional support for the whole family became my unspoken job. The mortgage was a worry but we kept up with payments. Travel restrictions cost us nothing financially, we just postponed trips.
