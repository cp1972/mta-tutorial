Q: How did you handle work or income during this period?
R: I think governments learned a lot but communicated badly at the start. Reconnecting with people after restrictions lifted was sometimes awkward. I waited a few weeks to see how the vaccines worked before taking mine. I was constantly cooking, the family ate every meal at home for months.

Q: What lessons do you take from this period?
R: The mental health consequences are still with us, I think, even now. I trusted the public health authorities even when their advice changed. I was advised to stay home for months because of my age and health. My salary was reduced for a while but my job was never really at risk.

Q: How did you experience the lockdown periods?
R: The vaccine appointment day was a real relief, almost a celebration. I learned who my real friends were when we could not meet in person. We postponed buying a new car and a few other expenses.
