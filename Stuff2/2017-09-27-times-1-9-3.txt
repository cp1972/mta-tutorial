Informa, the events and publishing company led by Lord Carter of Barnes, has bought Dove Medical Press. The FTSE 100 company said that the purchase of the open access journals publisher would increase choice for researchers across its subject areas. It expands Informa's total number of journals to more than 2,500. Dove's titles include International Journal of Nanomedicine and Clinical Ophthalmology. Informa's shares closed down 6½p at 664p.
